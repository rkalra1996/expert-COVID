What is COVID-19 ?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

How to prevent ?

Use Ethanol / Alcohol based Hand Sanitizers whenever you are in a public place.
Make sure to use it atleast once in every 30 to 40 minutes.

If you don't have sanitizers you can maintain your Hygine by whasing hands under running water with a soap for atleast 20 seconds.
This can be done everytime you are dealing with objects which involve too much physical contact.

Use a Face Mask (Atleast Layer 3 or N95) whenever going out in public and even indoors if you think you have symptoms of COVID-19.
Covering your mouth with Face mask will reduce the spread of coronavirus effectively.

Although it is recommended to stay at home during COVID-19 pandemic times, if you have to go out make your you practice social distancing by maintaining a minimum distance of 1 mtr
between two people and strictly avoiding public transport or places where people can gather.
